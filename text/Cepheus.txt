Fun Facts about Cepheus:
-It is one of the constellations in the northern sky
-It was first named as a constellation by 2nd-century astronomer Ptolemy
-It is named after King Cepheus of Aethiopia, from Greek mythology
-In Greek mythology, King Cepheus of Aethiopia, his wife Cassiopeia, and his daughter Andromeda were all immortalized as constellations
-Many people find Cepheus by looking for a cluster of stars near Cassiopeia that form the shape of a house
