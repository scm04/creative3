Fun Facts about Aries:
-It is one of the zodiac constellations
-It was first named as a constellation by 2nd-century astronomer Ptolemy
-It is located between Pisces to the west and Taurus to the east
-Its name is Latin for "ram"
-It is the 39th-largest constellation out of the 88 modern constellations
-It has represented a ram since late Babylonian times
-Over time, it has come to specifically represent the ram whose fleece became the Golden Fleece in Greek mythology
-In ancient Egyptian astronomy, Aries was associated with the god Amon-Ra, the god of fertility and creativity
