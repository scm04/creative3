Fun Facts about Gemini:
-It is one of the zodiac constellations
-It was first named as a constellation by 2nd-century astronomer Ptolemy
-Its name is Latin for "twins"
-It is associated with the twins Castor and Pollux in Greek mythology
-Gemini is the zodiac sign from May 21st to June 21st each year
-The sun's path actually crosses Gemini's location from June 20th to July 20th each year, hence why it is a zodiac constellation
