Fun Facts about Orion:
-It is visible throughout most of the world due to its location near the Earth's equator
-Due to its visibility, it is one of the most recognizable constellations
-It is named for the hunter Orion from Greek mythology
-The most visible stars in Orion are the three stars in his belt
-The earliest depiction of Orion was found on a mammoth ivory carving from a prehistoric culture
-Due to its wide recognition, Orion has many myths associated with it
