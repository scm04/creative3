<p>Fun Facts about Orion:</p>
<ul>
<li>It is visible throughout most of the world due to its location near the Earth's equator</li>
<li>Due to its visibility, it is one of the most recognizable constellations</li>
<li>It is named for the hunter Orion from Greek mythology</li>
<li>The most visible stars in Orion are the three stars in his belt</li>
<li>The earliest depiction of Orion was found on a mammoth ivory carving from a prehistoric culture</li>
<li>Due to its wide recognition, Orion has many myths associated with it</li>
</ul>
